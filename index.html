Q & A

Why do I need a website?

One main reason is that people expect to be able to interact with you even when they are not at your physical location. Whether this be browsing your products, reading about your company's values, or just getting into contact with you, if people cannot easily access information about you, they will look elsewhere.

Why do I need an app?

We are the mobile generation. Apps serve so many functions such as providing general information about what your company goals are, showing daily news updates, and gaining more visibility to your clients. With an app, you can expand your market coverage and gain the competitive advantage.

Why is IDEV best for me?

We know it can be hard to choose a company to create a website or app for you. There are so many of us after all. We aren't the biggest or oldest company on the block, but we know how to learn and listen. We'll start a project not by showing you slides, but understanding what you and your users want the most.

What if I don't really know what I want?

That's normal! It isn't realistic to know exactly how you want the website to look from the beginning because the best website possible is a product of the entire process. Fill out our questionnaire and let us know your business goals and expectations for the site. We'll help you figure this out.

What is the full range of IDEV's services?

IDEV can help you design and develop a website or mobile app. We also do SharePoint and ASPX.net development. If you need to migrate a website or an app, we provide this service as well. IDEV also offers systems and network administration services including infrastructure support, building servers (setting up exchange for emails, setting up VPN, and Citrix Servers), updating and upgrading servers, network engineering, and website hosting. If there's something you want but don't see here, feel free to contact us. We'll let you know.

What does IDEV not do?

While we do provide marketing services like SEO, we are not a dedicated marketing company. If you need large scale marketing or commercial advertising, we recommend you find a dedicated professional marketing firm.

How much will this cost?

We know some of the biggest hurdles in web and app development are the high prices. We want to lower that hurdle as much as we can. Pricing shouldn't be so painful or scary. A client that stays with IDEV means much more to us than a contract with a huge price tag, so getting a price quote from us is easy. Just fill out our questionnaire, and we'll report back with more details within three days.

So I pay the amount upfront and then you guys just start working?

No. We don't see why clients need to pay an upfront price on a project that will take a long time to finish. Instead, every client will be able to create a payment plan with us so there are periodic payments along the development process.

How long will the app or website take to finish?

After completing our questionnaire, you can expect to hear an initial report back from us within three days. Depending on the size of your request, and the goals outlined by your answers to our questionnaire, development time can take anywhere from two weeks for medium sized endeavors to three months for the grandest projects.

How much creative control will I have over my app or website?

IDEV understands that there will inevitably be disagreements on how a website or app should look. However, we also understand that we are helping you create your own vision. While we will adhere to our values of usability and clarity, your word is the ultimate decision.

Sounds great. So how do I start?

Even if you do not feel entirely ready, you should fill out our brief questionnaire that outlines your goals and expectations for this website or app. What do you have to lose? (sound warning!) You will hear back in less than three days with more details. If you still have any questions, great, you are a normal person. Feel free to contact us.
